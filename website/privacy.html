Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 1, 2026

Your Data Stays on Your Device

Helm is built with a local-first architecture. All workout logs, progress photos, body measurements, and personal records are stored directly on your device. We do not have access to your fitness data and cannot retrieve it from our servers because it never leaves your phone.

No Tracking or Analytics

The Helm app does not include any user tracking, analytics SDKs, or telemetry. We do not monitor how you use the app, which screens you visit, or how often you work out. Your habits are yours alone.

No Data Selling

We do not sell, rent, license, or otherwise share your personal data with third parties. Period. Your information is not a product.

No Advertising SDKs

Helm does not contain any advertising software development kits (SDKs) or ad networks. You will never see targeted ads in the app, and no data is collected for advertising purposes.

Email Collection via Website

If you choose to provide your email address through our website (for example, to be notified when the app launches), your email is stored securely and used solely for the purpose of sending you launch notifications and important product updates. We will never share your email with third parties or send unsolicited marketing.

Optional Cloud Sync (Coming Soon)

We plan to offer an optional cloud sync feature in the future. If you opt in, your data will be encrypted before it leaves your device and stored in encrypted cloud storage. Cloud sync will always be optional, and you can disable it at any time to return to fully local storage.

Data Export and Deletion

You can export all of your data at any time in a standard format. You can also delete all your data from the app at any time. Since your data is stored locally, deleting the app removes your data from your device entirely.

Children's Privacy

Helm is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you through the app or on our website. Continued use of Helm after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact us at helmfitness@outlook.com.
