Bodybuilding federations

NPC – The largest amateur bodybuilding federation in the USA and the main route to the IFBB Pro League.

IFBB Pro League – The professional bodybuilding federation that runs the Mr Olympia and other major pro shows.

MuscleContest – One of the biggest promoters of NPC and IFBB Pro League competitions across the United States.

GBO – International bodybuilding federation running amateur and professional competitions worldwide.

Natural bodybuilding federations

WNBF – International natural bodybuilding federation offering professional status for drug-tested athletes.

NANBF – North American Natural Bodybuilding Federation and amateur pathway to WNBF Pro status.

ANBF – American Natural Bodybuilding Federation promoting drug-tested amateur competitions.

INBA / PNBA – International natural bodybuilding federation known for strict drug testing and world championships.

OCB – Large US natural bodybuilding federation offering qualification for OCB professional status.

DFAC – Drug Free Athletes Coalition natural federation with amateur and professional competitions.

Fitness model federations

WBFF – Fitness model federation combining physique competition with model-style stage presentation.

Musclemania – Long-running fitness model federation focused on aesthetic physiques.

NFF – National Fitness Federation offering physique, fitness model and natural bodybuilding divisions.

This list is routinely updated and edited to be relevant to Charlies clients for UK comps only.

More competitions and dates are going ahead and added. This is a guide only.

Please refer to the specific Federation’s website for confirmation & details.
